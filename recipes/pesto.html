Ingredients

  • Basil
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Pine nuts
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Grab a food processor
  2. Add the basil, garlic, pine nuts, lemon juice, and salt and pepper
  3. Start grinding
  4. Slowly drip olive oil as you grind
  5. Stop when you're satisfied with the texture
